NORTHSIDE YOUTH SOCCER LEAGUE

Game Rules and Policies


FIFA rules will govern the game of NYSL except as modified in this document.

  • Sportsmanship

  • The common interest shared by the members of the Association is to inspire young people to practice the ideals of sportsmanship and fair play. Any player, coach, team, parent, spectator, administrator or referee whose behavior detracts from this purpose is subject to disciplinary action regardless of soccer technical background, experience, achievements or position.


  • FIFA Field Rules

        • 1.Dimensions

        FIFA Law 1 provides for field dimensions flexible external within maximum and minimum width and length given. Dimensions must be met for all fields used by teams under 12 years of age or older.

        2.Fields of competition

        The fields used within the program competition must be a minimum of 100 x 60 yards. The leagues, districts or associations participating in the state competition program who cannot provide a field that meets these requirements minimums must advise the State Competition Board that will assign your Field Committee to inspect the field and recommend to the Board if must grant or not an exemption from the minimum dimensions. Teams leagues, districts, or associations that cannot provide a field acceptable they may be required to play all their games away.

        3.Fields used by young age groups

        a. U-6 plays on a field of approximately 20 X 40 yards without penalty areas. Fields for older age groups they should be progressively larger.

        b. The U-8 plays on a field 40-50 yards long and 20-30 yards Wide.

        c. U-10 plays on a field 70-80 yards long and 40-50 yards long. width.

        d. Also, the fields used by the young age groups can have its internal dimensions and the size of the target similarly modified.


  • Everybody Plays

  • Everyone plays means that each recreational player on each team plays at least fifty percent of each game. It is encouraged to coaches in the competition program to play their players on 50% of each game. o A Coach will be allowed not to make a player play 50% of a play in special circumstances, for example absences unjustified practices and / or games, rude conduct in practice and / or games, and injuries.
